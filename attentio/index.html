Attentio Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Attentio is a Pomodoro and deep work productivity application. This Privacy Policy explains how Attentio handles information when you use the app.

Information We Access

Attentio may access the following on your device:

  • Notification permission, to alert you when focus or break sessions end.
  • Local app storage, to save your session history, statistics, and preferences.

How Your Data Is Used

Attentio uses data only to provide its core features, including:

  • Running focus and break timer cycles.
  • Saving completed sessions and generating productivity statistics.
  • Saving app preferences such as durations, theme, language, and sound settings.
  • Showing timer-completion notifications when enabled by you.

Data Collection and Sharing

Attentio does not require account registration and does not collect personal data in the current version.

Attentio does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Local Storage

Session history, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to our servers.

User-Initiated Sharing

If you choose to use platform-level sharing features outside of Attentio, content is shared only with the destination app or service you choose.

Children's Privacy

Attentio is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

SmartScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

SmartScan is a barcode and QR code scanner application. This Privacy Policy explains how SmartScan handles information when you use the app.

Information We Access

SmartScan may access the following on your device:

  • Camera, to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • Images or files selected by you, to scan codes from photos or documents.
  • Device storage only as needed to save generated code images when you request it.

How Your Data Is Used

SmartScan uses data only to provide its core features, including:

  • Scanning QR codes and barcodes.
  • Importing images or files for code detection.
  • Saving scan history and favorites locally on your device.
  • Generating, saving, and sharing digital versions of scanned codes when requested by you.

Data Collection and Sharing

SmartScan does not collect personal data and does not send your scan history, images, files, or scanned content to our servers.

SmartScan does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Local Storage

Scan history, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to us.

User-Initiated Sharing

If you choose to use sharing features in the app, the selected content is shared only with the destination app or service you choose.

Children's Privacy

SmartScan is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

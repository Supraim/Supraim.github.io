World Radio Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

World Radio is a radio streaming application that allows users to listen to live radio stations from around the world. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Access

World Radio may access the following on your device:

  • Internet connection, to stream radio stations.
  • Local storage, to save your preferences and favorite stations.
  • Notification permission (optional), to provide playback controls or updates.

How Your Data Is Used

World Radio uses data only to provide core functionality, including:

  • Streaming live radio stations.
  • Saving user preferences such as theme, language, and settings.
  • Saving favorite radio stations locally on your device.

Data Collection and Sharing

World Radio does not require account registration and does not collect personal data directly from users.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

  • Google AdMob (for ads). AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can learn more here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Radio Content and Third-Party Ownership

World Radio does not host, own, or control any radio streams or content available in the app. All radio stations, audio streams, and associated logos are provided by third-party sources.

All trademarks, logos, and audio content belong to their respective owners. World Radio is a directory and player that provides access to publicly available streams.

Local Storage

Favorites and app settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

World Radio is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Data Deletion Requests

Although World Radio does not require user accounts or collect directly identifiable personal information, users may request deletion of any data associated with advertising identifiers or app usage collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob).

To request deletion of data, users can contact us at: supraim.dev@gmail.com

When submitting a request, please include:

  • The name of the app (World Radio)
  • The request for data deletion
  • Optional: any additional details to help identify the request

We will process valid requests within a reasonable timeframe (typically up to 30 days).

Note: Some data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob) may be retained according to their own retention policies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact: supraim.dev@gmail.com
